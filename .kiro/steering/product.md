# Product Overview

Coffee Pickup API - A clean, fast MVP for browsing coffee items from cafe locations, customizing orders, and scheduling pickups.

## Core Features

- User authentication with JWT
- Cafe location browsing and selection
- Coffee menu with customization options (size, milk type, sweetness, extras)
- Shopping cart management (single cafe location per cart)
- Pickup time scheduling (15-minute intervals, minimum 15 minutes ahead)
- Order placement with mock payment
- Order history and tracking

## Key Constraints

- Pickup only (no delivery)
- Single cafe location per cart
- Orders must be placed at least 15 minutes in advance
- Maximum 10 items per order line
- Special instructions limited to 200 characters
